The Story Behind The Journey

This isn't just a release schedule - it's a documented transformation. From the money-obsessed survival mentality of "The Grind" to the love and vulnerability of "The Opening," each phase represents a fundamental shift in perspective and artistry.

The journey spans four distinct narrative arcs: survival and hustle, self-discovery and questioning, love and human connection, and finally, integrated wisdom. It's more than music - it's a year of life, growth, and artistic evolution distilled into sound.

"The best time to plant a tree was 20 years ago. The second best time is now."
